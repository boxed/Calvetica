How do I get Google calendar events to show up?

  1. Click here to visit Google's configuration page
  2. If prompted, sign into your Google account
  3. Tap the checkboxes of the calendars you want to see on your device
  4. Tap the save button at the bottom of the page
  5. The calendars should appear in the calendar menu of Calvetica. They may not show up immediately in Calvetica but will after a few minutes.

How do I add an event?

  1. Tap on the "+" icon in the red toolbar
  2. Use the keyboard to type your event name
  3. Use the numeric keypad to type your starting time
  4. Tap on the end time if you want to change the end time from its default amount
  5. Tap "Add Event" to add immediately or tap "More" to get more options for your event

How do I use reminders?

  1. Tap on the checkmark icon in the red toolbar to switch to reminder view
  2. Tap on the day where you'd like to add a new reminder
  3. Tap the "+" icon in the black toolbar
  4. Choose your reminder settings
  5. Tapping "Add" will add the new event. Tapping "Add +" will allow you to add more than one reminder without closing the add reminder dialog

How do I add a quick reminder?

  1. Tap and hold (long tap) on the "+" icon in the red toolbar
  2. In the quick reminder dialog, you can give your reminder a name and choose from the time span options which will add the reminder and close the dialog

How do I make the app icon show the date?

Apple doesn't allow us to change the icon itself but you can still show the date with an icon badge by following these instructions

  1. Tap on the view icon in the red toolbar (the three bar icon) and choose "Settings" from the popup.
  2. In the settings screen that appears, under the "Notifications" section, tap on "Icon Badge and Alerts"
  3. Choose "Day of Month Badge Only" or "Badge and Alerts" if you also want to use the custom Calvetica alerts

How do I add an all day event?

  1. Tap on the "+" icon in the red toolbar
  2. Use the keyboard to type your event name
  3. Tap "More" to get more options for your event
  4. Tap the "All Day" option
  5. Save your event

How do I delete an event?

  1. Swipe on the event you'd like to remove
  2. Tap on the "trash" icon that appears next to the event

How do Calvetica custom alerts work?

Because of the way Apple does system alerts with calendars, there's no way for us to turn off the built-in alerts. Our custom alerts are configured to fire 15 seconds after the Apple alert. If you wait for the Calvetica alert to appear, you'll have the option of "snoozing" the alert until later.

Using our custom alerts also makes it so you can use our custom sounds, some of which are louder and longer than the build-in Apple alert sound.
